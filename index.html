Chen Yu              

PhD student in the Computer Vision and Robotic Perception Laboratory at NUS.

I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate in the National University of Singapore and I'm advised by Prof. Gim Hee Lee. I worked at Segway Robotics during 2020.07-2021.12, where I designed algorithms to enhance the robustness of mapping algorithms for canteen robots and low-speed autonomous driving cars. Before that, I obtained my M.S. degree in Electronic Engineering and Computer Science School, Peking University, where I was advised by Prof. Yisong Chen and co-supervised by Prof. Shuhan Shen. I also spent four years at Beihang University, where I majored in software engineering. Now, I'm eager to tackle the 3D computer vision problem by combining deep learning and geometric methods.
