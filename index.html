Chen Yu              

PhD student in the Computer Vision and Robotic Perception Laboratory at NUS.

I'm currently a 1st year Ph.D student in the National University of Singapore and I'm advised by Prof. Gim Hee Lee. I worked at Segway Robotics during 2020.07-2021.12, where I designed algorithms to enhance the robustness of mapping algorithms for canteen robots and low-speed autonomous driving cars. Before that, I obtained my M.S. degree in Electronic Engineering and Computer Science School, Peking University, where I was advised by Prof. Yisong Chen and co-supervised by Prof. Shuhan Shen. I also spent 4 years in Beihang University, where I majored in software engineering. Now, I'm eager to tackle 3D computer vision problem by combining deep learning and geometric methods. (Never ask me to send your CVs to Gim Hee or anything related to that since I do not know you at all !)
